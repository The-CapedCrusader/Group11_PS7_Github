Frequently Asked Questions

1. How are products graded?

Products are graded based on a set of industry standards, including size, quality, and freshness. Each product category (e.g., fruits, vegetables, grains) has specific grading criteria.

2. How can I track my product?

You can track your product's status by clicking on the "Track" button available in the "My Products" section.

3. What do the grading statuses mean?

- **Completed:** The grading process is finished, and the product is ready for transportation.
- **In Progress:** The grading process is underway.
- **Pending:** The product is waiting to be graded.

4. How is transportation managed?

Transportation is managed through our logistics partners, ensuring timely and safe delivery. You can view the estimated delivery date in the "Shipping and Logistics" section.

5. What should I do if there is a delay in delivery?

If there is a delay in delivery, you will receive an alert in the "Notifications and Alerts" section. You can contact support for further assistance.

6. Can I download reports for my products?

Yes, you can download detailed grading and transportation reports from the "Download Reports" option in the Quick Links section.

7. How do I contact support?

You can contact support via email at support@foodgrading.com or through the "Contact Support" option on the Help & Support page.
